Welcome to Medical Practice Solutions - Your Partner in EHR Excellence

At Medical Practice Solutions, we are dedicated to alleviating the challenges that healthcare professionals face in transitioning to Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems. Founded by a Doctor of Medicine (MD) with a deep understanding of both medical practice and technology, we empathize with the struggles that physicians encounter in adapting to digital documentation.

Our Journey

After earning a Doctorate Degree from UNIBE, I returned to my hometown in Ponce, Puerto Rico, with the goal of conquering the USMLE exam. However, unforeseen circumstances, including a hurricane that disrupted exam schedules, compelled me to take on a different path. Balancing the responsibilities of a new family and the need to support them, I found myself working at a respected medical office. As time progressed, I seamlessly evolved into a multifaceted role, supporting the doctor in various capacities, from medical assistance and customer service to managing office workflows.

Recognizing the Need

During this time, I witnessed the growing concern among healthcare professionals regarding the shift towards EHR systems. Particularly, doctors, many of whom were not well-versed in technology, struggled to navigate the complexities of these systems. The challenges ranged from adapting to new interfaces to grappling with transcription tools that failed to capture nuances such as the distinct Puerto Rican English accent.

Empowering Excellence

Driven by my passion for technology and my commitment to enhancing medical practice, I ventured into founding Medical Practice Solutions. Our mission is to empower medical practitioners, especially those in the later stages of their careers, with seamless EHR solutions. Our services extend beyond conventional transcription. We provide comprehensive support in navigating EHR intricacies, from system orientation to data entry, ensuring that doctors can focus on patient care rather than wrestling with technology.
